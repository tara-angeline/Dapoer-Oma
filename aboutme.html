About Me

Meet the writer behind "Dapoer Oma"!

  • Home
  • About Me

Hi there! Nice to meet you :)

Hello, I'm Tara and Dapoer Oma is little corner in this big internet!

Originally from the vibrant archipelago of Indonesia, I'm an international student passionate about sharing the rich culinary heritage of my homeland. Growing up surrounded by a tapestry of flavors and aromas, I've always been captivated by the art of Indonesian cooking. Each dish tells a story, reflecting the diverse cultures, traditions, and landscapes that shape our nation.

I have gotten the oppurtunity to study aboard in University College Dublin, studying in Computer Science.

As I navigate life abroad, I find comfort and connection through the recipes that remind me of home. Through this website, I aim to introduce you to the authentic tastes of Indonesia, offering a curated collection of traditional and modern recipes that celebrate our unique culinary identity.

This website is also my way of honoring the incredible women in my family, my mom and grandma, by sharing their culinary legacy with the world. It's a tribute to their love, patience, and the countless hours spent together in the kitchen, crafting meals that bring joy and togetherness to our family table.

Join me on this gastronomic journey as we explore the vibrant spices, fresh ingredients, and heartfelt traditions that make Indonesian cuisine truly special. Whether you're a seasoned chef or new to Indonesian cooking, there's something here for everyone to enjoy.

Terima kasih for visiting and Selamat Makan! :)
