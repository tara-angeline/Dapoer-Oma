Beef Rendang

  • Time: 3 hours

  • Servings: 10 serves

  • Category: Main

INGREDIENTS

  • 1kg beef shank/beef chuck, cubed

  • 500ml coconut milk

  • 4 kaffir lime leaves

  • 3 sticks lemongrass, bruised

  • 4 pieces asam kandis/tamarind

  • 100ml cooking oil

  • 5 grams salt

  • 60 grams shallot

  • 50 grams garlic

  • 250 grams red chilies

  • 100 grams galangal

  • 50 grams candlenut

  • 2 grams cloves

INSTRUCTION

  1. Grind shallot, garlic, chillies, galangal, candlenut, and cloves in a food processor until a paste forms.

  2. Heat oil in a wok/large pot over a medium-high heat and fry the spice paste until fragrant.

  3. Add in kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, and salt. Fry about 3 minutes, keep stiring.

  4. Add beef cubes, stir until no longer pink.

  5. Add coconut milk and asam kandis. Mix well and bring to a boil.

  6. Reduce heat to medium heat and leave the wok/pot uncovered. Cook until the liquid is reduced and beef is tender (about 3 hours).

  7. Once liquid thickens and darkens, take it off the heat. Serve with steamed rice.
