Soto Betawi

  • Time: 1.5 hours

  • Servings: 5 serves

  • Category: Main

INGREDIENTS

  • 500grams beef brisket, cubed

  • 250grams beef tripe

  • 2 litres water

  • 250ml whole milk

  • 250ml coconut milk

  • 2.5 teaspoon salt

  • 1 teaspoon pepper

  • 1 tablespoon sugar

  • 2 tomatoes, cut into pieces

  • 2 potatoes, diced and fried

  • Cooking oil, for frying/sauteeing

Spice Paste:

  • 10 pieces shallots

  • 5 cloves garlic

  • 1/2 inch ginger

  • 2 red chillies

  • 3 pieces candlenut

  • 1 teaspoon coriander

  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin

Additional Spices:

  • 2 Indonesian Bay leaves

  • 2 sticks lemongrass

  • 5 kaffir lime leaves

  • 1 inch galangal

  • 2 pieces cloves

  • 1 piece star anies

  • 2 piece cinnamon

INSTRUCTION

  1. Grind spice paste ingredients in a food processor until a paste forms. Set aside.

  2. Heat oil in a pan and stir fry the spice paste until fragrant.

  3. Add the additional spices. Stir fry for about 3 minutes. Set aside

  4. Heat water in a pot over a medium-high heat and boil beef tripe with kaffir lime leaves until tender. Set aside and cut into smaller pieces.

  5. Heat water in a pot over a medium-high heat and boil beef cubes until tender. Remove any impurities from the water.

  6. Add cooked beef tripe and spices into the broth.

  7. Season with salt, sugar, and pepper.

  8. Once the beef has absorbed the flavours of the spices, add coconut milk and whole milk. Bring the liquid to a boil, then turn off the heat.

  9. Serve with tomato slices and fried potato cubes.
